{\LARGE {\textbf{Symbol Conventions}}}

  \vspace{9mm}

Through out the thesis following Symbol Convention has been used.\\[4mm]

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{K} \hspace{5mm} a field.
\item \textbf{F} \hspace{5mm} an extension field of the field \textbf{K}
\end{itemize}
